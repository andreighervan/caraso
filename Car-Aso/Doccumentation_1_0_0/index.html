How To Change Image

It is super easy. You need to open Photoshop. Go to Windown > Layers (F7) to show the Layers Panel.

In The Layers Panel, Double click the Layer Icon and change your Image

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

How To Turn On/Off Group/Layers

It is super easy. You need to open Photoshop. Go to Windown > Layers (F7) to show the Layers Panel.

In The Layers Panel, Click The Eye Icon. You will turn on Groups/Layers. Enjoy.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

How To Edit Text

Open your Photoshop file.

Click the Horizontal Type Tool (T) in the tool box. Click on one of the existing letters or numbers in your text and edit it.
